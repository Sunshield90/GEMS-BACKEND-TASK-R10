How to Use GEMSTask

Getting Started

Welcome to GEMSTask! This guide will walk you through the main features of the application.

1. Authentication

First, you need an account. You can either Sign Up to create a new account or Log In if you already have one. Authentication is required to access the dashboard and manage tasks.

2. The Dashboard

This is your main workspace. It displays all current tasks as cards. You can see the task title, description, status (Pending, In-Progress, Completed), and its due date at a glance.

  • Adding a Task: Click the "Add Task" button on the dashboard to open a form. Fill in the details and assign it to any registered user.
  • Editing a Task: On each task card, click the pencil icon () to open the form with the task's current details, which you can then modify.
  • Deleting a Task: Click the trash can icon () on a task card to permanently remove it. A confirmation will be required.

3. User Management

Click on the "Users" link in the sidebar to see a list of all registered users in the system, along with their registration date.

4. Logging Out

You can log out securely by clicking the exit icon () next to your name in the bottom-left corner of the sidebar. This will clear your session and return you to the login screen.
